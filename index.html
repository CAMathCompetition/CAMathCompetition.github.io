Cheshire Academy Math Competition

Cheshire Academy Math Competition(CAMC) is held by Cheshire Academy Math Club members. We provide a team competition for more middle school students to participate in both geographically and mathematically, and promote communication among mathematics enthusiasts from across the country.

Learn more »

Who We Are

    

 CA Math Club is a club run by a group of people who love math at Cheshire Academy. The members wanted to provide a team-based competition for schools to help participants learn and enjoy math as they participated.

 

Participation Detail 

  • We encourage all math enthusiasts to participate.
  • 2023 CAMC is being held in person at Cheshire Academy in March 2023.
  • Each team participating in the competition can have 1 - 5 participants.

    • Contest Format

    How does the contest process 

        The individual round is a 15 questions, 75 minutes test. All of the questions are weighted equally and the diffculty will increase. 

        The team round is a 10 questions, 45 minutes test. All questions are weighted equally, and you may work with your team to solve the problems. 

        The awards will be seperated into Individual Award and Team Award.
